The Proposal

It all started with an eggplant (or aubergine for UK translation). The first time we met, Joe gave me this vegetable in a London bar, following my initial confusion with his chat-up line and getting over the fact that he was American, we got chatting, drinking and dancing...

A year later and after much partying, adventures in Europe and deciding that we kind of liked each other, a proposal came. A ring was produced from a remote control car, on bended knee. It was beautiful, perfect and very Joe. I am a very lucky lady.
