Location and Flights for UK Guests

The venue is located in Broad Brook, Connecticut. The closest airport is Bradley International in Hartford, CT. There are direct flights from London, Heathrow to JFk New York, Newark International or Logan International in Boston, where you would then get a connecting flight to Hartford.
